Hick's Law

interaction-design
www.interaction-design.org

On this page I really like how it was designed, it was my favorite of the three, because it makes good use of what it has, the titles with larger letters even in another color, then the text is well placed in the center without elements that distract to concentrate on the text, and as we advance on the page the title space disappears to concentrate more deeply on the text.

Fitts’s Law

lawsofux
lawsofux.com

The second is similar to the previous one, because it also leaves its space divided into parts such as the title, the images or the text, but unlike the previous one that was afraid to combine everything, here everything is separated or at least it feels like that, For example, the area of the title is too large to reach the content of the text, I don't see it badly, but it is unnecessary, it could have been used better.

Rule of Thirds

masterclass
masterclass.com

This is the one that I like the least of the three because here it does everything wrong, you cannot move freely on the page, there is no organization in the sizes of the icons and when you try to read it is very uncomfortable due to the large number of movements that take place must do to read a simple sentence.
